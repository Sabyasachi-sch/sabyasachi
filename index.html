PhD Student, Department Of Physics IIT Kharagpur
Prime Minister Research Fellow

I am Sabyasachi Chakraborty, a PMRF research scholar of the Department of Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. My research interest lies in the area of Quantum Computing. I am working under supervision of Prof. Sonjoy Majumder and collaborating with Dr. Bibhas Adhikari. I am working in quantum many-body phenomena, using numerical simulations to study complex quantum systems. My research focuses on non-equilibrium dynamics and parametrized quantum circuits. I am modelling quantum behaviours within the driven Bose-Hubbard framework and developing efficient quantum circuits for simulating complex many-body Hamiltonians on NISQ systems.
