Research Experience

Hamiltonian simulation and Investigation of Quantum information scrambling

August 2022 - Present

PhD Research, IIT Kharagpur

  • Implementing an optimization-based method for comparing a given unitary matrix to a generic parametrized unitary matrix in order to approximate it and to simulate time evolution of Hamiltonian.
  • Studying Information scrambling to test it on the parametrized unitaries.

Quantum Properties of bosonic mixture in optical lattice

August 2021 – May 2022

MTech Research, IIT Kharagpur

  • Developed and numerically implemented a time-dependent Gutzwiller approach on the Bose-Hubbard model which identifies the quantum phases in symmetric & non-symmetric binary mixture of ultracold bosons in optical lattices.
  • Expanded this time-dependent Gutzwiller approach on the Bose-Hubbard model to investigate the dynamics for an inhomogeneous system with a fixed total number of atoms and external harmonic confinement.

Astronomical Photometric Observations and Data Reduction

Research Project at Fr. Eugene Lafont Observatory, St. Xavier’s College Kolkata
